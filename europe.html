Study in Europe

Studying in Europe provides students with a unique and enriching experience, with opportunities for personal and academic growth. With high-quality education, a welcoming society, and excellent post-study work opportunities, Europe is an excellent choice for international students seeking a well-rounded education and a bright future.

Why study in Europe?

Europe is one of the most popular destinations for international students, offering high-quality education, a welcoming society, and beautiful landscapes. In addition to this, Europe also offers excellent post-study work opportunities for graduates.

Graduates with a bachelor's, master's, or doctoral degree from an Europen university can apply for a post-study work visa, allowing them to stay in the country and gain work experience related to their field of study. This visa can range from two to four years, potentially leading to permanent residency and long-term career prospects.

Cost of studying in Europe for international students

Europe is a popular destination among international students for higher education due to its world-renowned universities and high-quality education system. The cost of studying in Europe for international students varies depending on the institution and the course they choose. On average, tuition fees for undergraduate courses range from 12,000 to 15,000 AUD per semester.

Apart from tuition fees, international students are also required to bear living expenses, including accommodation, food, and transportation. According to estimates, the average living expense for international students in Europe is around 21,041 AUD per year. Additionally, students may also need to consider travel expenses, which can cost around 2,200 AUD.

However, one of the advantages of studying in Europe is that international students are allowed to work part-time up to 24 hours per week while studying. This can help cover their living and travel expenses while also gaining work experience.

Scholarships for international students in Europe

Europe is a popular study destination for international students due to its high-quality education system and multicultural environment. One of the biggest concerns for students studying abroad is the cost of tuition fees. However, Europe offers various scholarships to international students to make their study abroad journey affordable and accessible.

The scholarship amount can vary depending on the course and university. International students can receive up to 30% off their tuition fees and living expenses.

Post-Study Work Opportunities

Europe offers excellent post-study work opportunities for international students who wish to stay in the country after completing their studies. Students who have completed a Bachelor's degree can apply for a Post-Study Work (PSW) visa, which allows them to stay and work in Europe for up to 2-4 years, depending on their chosen field of study. Similarly, students who have completed a Master's degree can apply for a PSW visa that is valid for up to 3-5 years. These PSW visas provide international students with an opportunity to gain valuable work experience, earn money, and develop their careers in Europe. The post-study work opportunities in Europe make it an attractive destination for international students who are looking to pursue higher education and build their careers.
